Research and engineering projects in soft robotics and actuator design.

PhD Research · Empa / EPFL

Biocompatible Ionic Polymer Actuators via 4D Printing

Designed and fabricated ionic polymer metal composite (IPMC) actuators using biocompatible materials and multi-material 4D printing. Developed magnetically driven actuation mechanisms achieving high-power output for underwater soft robots.

→ Spotlight paper at RoboSoft 2025 · Cover of Advanced Sustainable Systems

Project photo / GIF
MSc Research · UEC Tokyo

Biodegradable & Edible Soft Robots

Developed biodegradable soft robotic systems using edible and eco-safe materials. Designed an electrohydraulic pump mechanism for soft actuation without conventional rigid components.

→ Best Paper Finalist, RoboSoft 2024

BSc Research · UEC Tokyo

Rapid Fabrication of Electroactive Polymer Actuators

Developed rapid fabrication methods for electroactive polymer actuators. Also worked on paper-based electronics using silver nanowire networks for flexible and lightweight sensor applications.
