Ryo Kanno

Soft Actuator Design & Sustainable Robotics

PhD Candidate · Laboratory of Sustainability Robotics, Empa & EPFL

I design and fabricate biocompatible ionic polymer actuators using 4D printing, with a focus on magnetically driven high-power underwater soft robots. My expertise spans soft material engineering, rapid prototyping, and actuation system design — bridging fundamental research with real-world robotic applications.
