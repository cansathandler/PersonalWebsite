Ryo Kanno

Soft Robotics · Biocompatible Actuators · 4D Printing

PhD Candidate · Laboratory of Sustainability Robotics, Empa & EPFL, Switzerland

I develop biocompatible ionic polymer actuators using 4D printing, with a focus on magnetically driven high-power underwater soft robots. My work sits at the intersection of materials science, soft robotics, and sustainable design, aiming to create robots that are safe for both humans and the environment.
