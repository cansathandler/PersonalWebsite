About

PhD candidate working on soft robotics and biocompatible ionic polymer actuators.

I am a PhD candidate at the Laboratory of Sustainability Robotics at Empa and EPFL in Switzerland. My doctoral research focuses on biocompatible ionic polymer actuators with 4D printing, developing magnetically driven high-power underwater soft robots.

I also work as a Doctoral Assistant at Empa, and previously served as a Student Teaching Assistant for the Sustainability Robotics course at EPFL (Spring 2025).
